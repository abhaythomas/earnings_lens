INDUSTRY GLOSSARY
Clinker Capacity: The maximum amount of intermediate product (clinker) a cement plant can produce, serving as a key determinant of potential cement output.
IIJA (Infrastructure Investment and Jobs Act): U.S. federal legislation providing funding for public infrastructure, driving construction demand in Titan America's markets.
Fly ash: A byproduct from coal-fired power plants used as a supplementary cementitious material in concrete production.
Net Leverage Ratio: The ratio of net debt to adjusted EBITDA, used to assess financial risk and borrowing capacity.
